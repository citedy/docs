# Credit Pricing

**Credit value**: 1 credit = $0.01 USD

## Credit Packages

| Package | Credits | Price | Per Credit | Discount |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 500 | $5.99 | $0.012 | 0% |
| Growth | 1,500 | $14.99 | $0.010 | 17% (popular) |
| Pro | 4,500 | $39.99 | $0.0089 | 26% |
| Business | 14,000 | $99.99 | $0.0071 | 29% |

New users receive **100 free credits** on registration.

## Insufficient Balance

If your balance is insufficient for an operation, you'll receive a `402` response with a link to top up credits. Failed operations are automatically refunded.
